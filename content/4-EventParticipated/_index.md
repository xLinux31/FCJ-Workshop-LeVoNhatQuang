### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)
&emsp;**Event Name:** Cloud Mastery AI From Scratch  

&emsp;**Date & Time:** 09:00, February 14, 2026  

&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City  

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee  

&emsp;**Main Content and Activities:**
- Presented the session “Automated Prompt Engineering: Enhancing LLM Output Quality”.
- Introduced the importance of prompts and how to build effective prompts (role, instruction, context, examples, constraints).
- Shared advanced techniques such as Chain-of-Thought, RAG, role prompting to improve quality and cost when using LLMs.
- Guided beginners on how to start building AI Agents.
- Introduced 2 projects:
   + Locker Management (AIoT): integrated AWS IoT Core, Amazon Rekognition, RFID, sensors, and cameras for device borrowing/return management.
   + Plutus: expense management application using Amazon Textract for smart invoice OCR processing.
- Participated in Q&A, discussed AI, Cloud, and IoT with students.

&emsp;**Outcomes or Value Gained:**
- Enhanced understanding of Prompt Engineering and effective LLM usage.
- Gained knowledge about AI Agents and integrating AI with Cloud (AWS).
- Developed listening, questioning, and critical thinking skills.
- Learned how to apply theory to real-world projects (AIoT, OCR, Cloud).
- Realized the importance of building real products instead of just learning theory.
